Positioning Statement

A narrative summary of the previous parts.

SALA/UBC is an entrepreneurial collaborative who support and study the design of solutions for complex issues facing the systems and structures of the world. Our location in Vancouver, on the west coast of Canada, has always driven our interest in sustainability, diversity, globalism and growth. Our entrepreneurial spirit has helped us bypass bureaucratic impediments, and have an outsized impact on the profession. It’s in our nature to collaborate internally, across disciplines, and throughout the communities with which we engage.
