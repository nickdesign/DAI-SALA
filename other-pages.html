What concerns us most?

We see a world far more complex than the one most people are even aware of. We see behind the surface, beyond obvious daily routines. We see the grand theatrical work of humanity as well as the trappings backstage; a system that supports billions of people as they live, work, and play.

We see an incredibly complex and interconnected system of systems where buildings, and the spaces between them form communities, neighbourhoods, and cities. These systems are alive: they grow and evolve and decay and die. They are a reflection of the lives and times that they make possible.

Our role is to study these systems, observe how they work, intervene with design solutions when it makes good sense to, and spread the word about what we see.
